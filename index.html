Welcome to Signal K

"I wish to communicate with you"

The Free and Open Source universal marine data exchange

SignalK is about publishing a common modern and open data format for marine use. A format for the modern boat, compatible to NMEA, friendly to wifi, cellphones, tablets, and the internet. A format available to every-one, where any-one can contribute.

To get started, see the summay page and have a look through Navgauge Issue #4 to get a feel for where we started. Then join the mailinglist by sending an email to signalk+subscribe@googlegroups.com or follow the discussion via the Signal K Google Groups forum.
